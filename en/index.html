Frequently Asked Questions

Is VAT included in pricing?
Yes, the prices mentioned in the website contain the VAT.
How will you issue invoices?
Himnark CJSC will issue invoices electronically in the beginning of the next month. The invoices will be billed to the individual or legal entity who concluded the contract with Himnark CJSC.
What happens if a customer does not activate a plan after free trial period?
Customer will have access to the system, but will not be able to enter or change any data there.
Is it possible to activate a plan for several companies?
Yes, it is possible to connect unlimited number of entities to your account.
How do you count the number of employees in the monthly invoice?
It is calculated according to the number of employees with active labor contracts in all entities associated with your account as of the last working day of that month.
How many users may access the system?
The number of users is unlimited

Features

Manage labor contracts

When onboarding, an employee user specifies the personal information, working schedule and the salary. The system will generate labor contracts and employee will be included in the payroll list.

Leave Pays

System automatically calculates sick and maternity leaves. User just needs to specify start and end dates of a leave.

Legislation changes

Changes of the legislation and regulations are automatically reflected in the system.

Payroll calculation

Payroll generation is fully automated. You just need to verify that all necessary HR records are made.

Overtime and night work

System calculates the overtime and night work pay based on the start and end hours such work.

Payment processing

Payment documents are generated according to the payment method preferred by the employee. Payment documents can be printed and imported into AS Accountant 7.

Vacations

You just need to set start and end date of vacation to generate vacation paperwork and calculate vacation pay. The system automatically calculates the number of available vacation days.

Documentation Management

Generate labor contracts and amendments, orders, applications and certificates and maintain the history of generated documents.

Data Import

Import employee data from a spreadsheet
